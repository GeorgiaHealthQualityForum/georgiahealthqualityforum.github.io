The Team

Our team members are committed to developing a profession around patient safety and quality improvement in healthcare through education, research, and advocacy on the conditions that promote safe clinical practices and prevent harm to patients. Please note that GHQF is a completely volunteer effort, and that we operate with no foreign or domestic funding.


Core Members

Peter F. Martelli, PhD, MSPH

Peter F. Martelli is Associate Professor of Healthcare Administration in the Sawyer Business School at Suffolk University. His research focuses on system safety and reliability. He holds a PhD from the University of California - Berkeley and was a Fulbright Scholar to Georgia from 2021-23.

Eka Rukhadze, MD, PhD

Eka Rukhadze is co-Head of the Department of Professional Development at Georgian American University, and Head of Quality and Patient Safety Department at Curatio Clinic in Tbilisi. She holds a PhD from David Tvildiani Medical University.

Nino Butskhrikidze, MD

Nino Butskhrikidze is Quality Management Specialist at New Hospitals Ltd. and co-Head of the Department of Professional Development at Georgian American University. In 2008, she led Tbilisi Central University Hospital in the first Georgian effort to formalize quality improvement. She is a co-founder of the Georgian Emergency Medicine Physicians Association.

Eka Cherkezishvili, MD, MPH

Eka Cherkezishvili is BMJ Georgia Implementation Lead and an Invited Lecturer in the Department of Public Health and Health Care Administration at the University of Georgia.

Lela Sturua, MD

Lela Sturua is a consultant and trainer in healthcare quality improvement and patient safety. She is a Lean Six Sigma Green Belt Healthcare Quality Improvement professional and is ISQua double-certified in External Evaluation Surveying and patient centered care. Lela is an Invited Lecturer at Tbilisi State University and Tbilisi Medical Academy on the subject of healthcare quality improvement and patient safety..

Giorgi Gabisonia, MD

George Gabisonia is Vice President for Educational Programs at Georgian American University.

Natalia Kalandarishvili, MD

Natalia Kalandarishvili is Chief Quality Officer at American Hospital Tbilisi.

Eka Paatashvili, MD, MPA

Eka Paatashvili is Senior Program Officer at the Sabin Vaccine Institute and an Assistant Professor in the School of Medicine at Ilia State University. From 2000-2012, she worked in health policy at the Georgia Ministry of Labour, Health and Social Affairs.

Sophio Beridze, MD

Sophio Beridze is head of the MD program and Professor at Batumi Shota Rustaveli State University, and the Rector of Avicenna–Batumi Medical University. As a transplant hepatologist, she was a member of the team that performed the first-ever liver transplantation in Georgia.

Tyler J. Towers, PhD, MHA

Tyler J. Towers is a Professor of Practice with adjunct faculty appointments at the University of Texas San Antonio (UTSA) and Texas Tech University Health Sciences Center (TTUHSC). He has over twenty years of professional experience working as a healthcare administrator in the United States Navy, holding various leadership positions in organizations across the United States and in Japan. He earned his PhD at the Pennsylvania State University and completed a Fulbright Specialist Project in Georgia in June 2024.
