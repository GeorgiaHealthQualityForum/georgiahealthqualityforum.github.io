Georgia Health Quality Forum


Our mission is to develop a profession around patient safety and quality improvement in healthcare through education, research, and advocacy on the conditions that promote safe clinical practices and prevent harm to patients, including:

  • Convening and coordinating discussion among a community of practitioners, public and private leaders, and academic researchers;
  • Promoting research and analysis on these topics in the Georgian context;
  • Developing and sharing resources, such as curricula, standards, and measurement tools; and,
  • Supporting undergraduate and continuing medical education in systems improvement.
