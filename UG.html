IHI UG CHAPTER

The Institute for Healthcare Improvement (IHI) is a global leader in advancing patient safety, quality improvement, and health system transformation through education, research, and collaboration. The IHI UG student Chapter is a dedicated academic initiative focused on enhancing healthcare quality, improving patient safety, and fostering a culture of continuous learning and innovation. As a proud collaborator with the Georgian Health Quality Forum, our mission is to equip future health professionals with the skills and knowledge necessary to identify and address system inefficiencies, reduce medical errors, and implement evidence-based improvements in healthcare. Through education, collaboration, and hands- on experiences, we empower students to become proactive leaders in shaping a safer, more effective healthcare system.
