From Cuts to Care: Cutting Through Errors

Date: December 21, 2024

On December 21st IHI Student Club in collaboration with INAMS team held an event 'From Cuts To Care: Cutting Through Errors'. The main subject of the event was Kidney and misdiagnosis in nephrology. During the event we discussed kidney anatomy and physiology, students were able to dissect and explore the kidney and gain practical experience. Furthermore, we discussed patient safety topics such as misdiagnosis in nephrology.

Suturing Competition

Date: October 4, 2024

The Knot Battle is an exciting and competitive suturing event designed to test the precision, speed, and technique of medical students and healthcare professionals. Participants faced off in multiple rounds, tackling increasingly complex suturing challenges. This competition is a true test of surgical dexterity, knot-tying mastery, and suturing finesse.
The competition is a pre-conference event for Surgx24, the international students' surgical conference. Organized by Edugradient.
Winners will receive free conference tickets!

Webinar: WPSD24

Date: September 18, 2024

In alignment with World Patient Safety Day 2024’s theme, “Improving Diagnosis for Patient Safety,” and under the slogan “Get it right, make it safe!,” we are excited to announce the World Patient Alliance (WPA) webinar on Diagnosis. This event brought together leading experts, patients, and patient advocates to explore innovative strategies, share best practices, and discuss how we can foster collaboration among healthcare providers, patients, and families to minimize diagnostic errors.

World Bicycle Day

Date: June 2, 2024

"Cycling for Health, Cycling Towards Europe" - The event highlights the dual benefits of physical activity for personal well-being and environmental sustainability. Since its inception in 2018, Cycling Day has been dedicated to raising awareness about the importance of active transportation and its positive impacts on health and the environment. The march was proudly organized by CCN, the WHO, and the Bicycle Mayor of Tbilisi in collaboration with the IHI student club.
